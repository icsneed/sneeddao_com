About Sneed DAO

A Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) is an organization that is governed by its members. DAOs operate with rules encoded in smart contracts, which removes the need for centralized leadership (e.g., a CEO).

On the Internet Computer Protocol (ICP), decentralized applications (dApps) can be controlled by SNS (Service Nervous System) DAOs. SNS DAOs are advanced DAOs that operate entirely on the ICP blockchain, enabling dApp operation and maintenance fully within Web3 through a token-based governance system.

Sneed DAO is a fully community-owned and community-driven SNS DAO on ICP, emerging from the legacy of SNS-1 (nicknamed "SNEED" by the community). It was designed as a customizable and open "Blank Canvas" dApp, with the goal of providing a self-sustaining platform for the ICP community to innovate and build.

Sneed DAO empowers its community members to propose, vote on, and develop any ideas, projects, partnerships, etc. There are no limits set. Sneed DAO aims to be an open and collaborative platform, inviting everyone—from developers and creatives to enthusiasts with ambitious ideas—to join, contribute, and build together.

Vision

The main vision of Sneed DAO is that its community members shape the future of the DAO using an open-platform "Blank Canvas" approach, which is realized through a straightforward three-step process using the SNEED token:

  1. Proposal Submission: Community developers and members submit proposals for new projects, initiatives, or changes (for example on OpenChat or Proposals.network). These proposals are then publicly votable on the NNS.
  2. DAO Decision: SNEED token holders vote on proposals.
  3. DAO Action: Approved proposals are implemented, dApp code changes are directly, trustlessly made on-chain via the SNS.

This "Blank Canvas" model stands for an "open call" to the ICP community for any kind of software development. Anyone can submit their proposal to the DAO. The future of Sneed DAO is 100% shaped by its decentralized community, which is in control of the DAO treasury and the DAO dApps.

For more details, read Mr. Sneed's Whiteletter to the Community.

SneedScan is a blockchain indexer and explorer for the Sneed token, developed by the Sneed DAO community. It allows users to view transaction histories and monitor token activity, a feature typically missing for comparable ICRC-1 community tokens.

SneedScan shows the Sneed vision in action: community-driven development and transparency.

SneedSwap was developed by the Sneed DAO community ahead of its SNS launch. SneedSwap allows for a trustless and permissionless token swap of ICRC-1 tokens to SNS token standards.

It was audited by RIVVIR Tech, LLC.

The Sneed Diamond Club is an exclusive holder's community within Sneed DAO and the first NFT-gated group that is synchronized between web2 (Discord) and web3 (Catalyze).

50% of the NFT collection was airdropped to SNEED token holders (>1 SNEED holders received 2 each), while the other 50% is stored in the Sneed DAO treasury for future incentives.

The collection is currently being traded on Yuku. 100% of the 3% Creator's Royalty is automatically transferred to the Sneed DAO treasury.

SneedLock 2.0 is a fully permissionless and trustless wallet on the ICP blockchain developed by the Sneed DAO community. It is the standalone dApp version of SneedLock 1.0, which is the first LP lock solution on ICP.

SneedLock 2.0 allows users (e.g., community token teams) to lock ICRC1 tokens and liquidity pools (LP) for specific durations.

SneedLock enhances security and trust within the ICP ecosystem by preventing access to locked tokens and/or LPs until the lock expires, helping to mitigate risks like "rug-pulls."

SneedLock is a modern alternative to "blackholing" LP positions, in which case the LP owners would forfeit claiming the LP rewards forever.

SneedLock supports an open API for integration with decentralized exchanges (DEXs) on ICP, with a primary focus on ICPSwap.

SneedLock 2.0 is currently in Beta.

Sneed DAO Tokenomics: RLL Model

The Recursive Liquidity Loop (RLL) is a model developed by the Sneed SNS DAO as an alternative to inflationary SNS tokens. The governance token of Sneed DAO (SNEED) is non-inflationary, meaning the total supply (10 000 SNEED) will never increase. Here is a short breakdown of how it works:

1. Burning/Distributing SNEED Tokens
Rewards generated in SNEED tokens from the Sneed DAO Liquidity Pools (LPs) are regularly burned (removed from circulation) and/or distributed to the Sneed DAO governance members. Sneed DAO proposals can change this ratio from 100% burned to 100% distributed. This burn/distribution process is a core part of the DAO's approach to managing the ecosystem because it avoids the issuance of new SNEED tokens.

2. Staking ICP Rewards
Rewards earned in ICP (Internet Computer Protocol) coins from the Sneed DAO LPs are staked in an 8-year neuron which is controlled by the DAO. This staking process helps the DAO participate in ICP's governance and earn additional rewards that support the DAO's activities.

3. Distributing Maturity Rewards from Staked ICP
As the staked ICP tokens generate returns, these are distributed back to the Sneed SNS DAO community as governance rewards. These governance rewards can then be spent on buying-back SNEED tokens and/or on the distribution to Sneed DAO governance members. This approach closes the RLL as buying-back SNEED tokens again generate rewards in Sneed DAO LPs.

The RLL model is designed to minimize token inflation, helping to maintain the integrity of DAO votes and retain DAO self-sustainability. Launch the Sneed DAO dApp to see the RLL model in action!
